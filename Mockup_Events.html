SPP-wide Events

To promote the cooperation and the knowlegde transfer within the SPP and with external partners and reviewers, we organise several workshops at regular intervals. These meetings serve for the intensive discussion on concepts and results of the projects. Furthermore we use these events for collaborations on joint case studies. In addition to the official events of SPP1593, there are several associated events, closely related to the research topics of the priority programme.
